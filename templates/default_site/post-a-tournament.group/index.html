Post A Tournament

Thank you for choosing to post your event with GolfTourneys.com. Follow these four easy steps to begin.

1. Log in.

You must log in with your GolfTourneys.com account before posting a golf tournament. If you do not have an account, click here to start your free membership then return to the POST A TOURNAMENT page.

2. Par or Birdie?

As the tournament organizer, you’ll decide between the free Par posting option and the low-cost Birdie option. Click here to learn more about the services and costs associated with each alternative.

3. Review Posting Tips.

Click here to learn about the golf tournament posting process and to read tips that will maximize the value of your tournament’s online description.

4. Get Started.

You’re ready to go! Click the button below to create your golf tournament.
